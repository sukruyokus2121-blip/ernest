FAQ

Questions, answered.

A few things teams usually ask before we start. Anything else — just reach out.

What do you build?
Blockchain and software products for crypto, DeFi, and fintech teams — the whole stack, from smart contracts to the web and mobile apps built on top of them.
Which chains do you work with?
EVM chains and Solana — including wallets, L2s, and the real-world UX of moving value on-chain.
How do engagements work?
Every project moves through four blocks — Discovery, Architecture, Build, and Ship — each reviewed and signed off before the next begins, so there are no surprises at launch.
How do you approach security?
Security is the default, not a phase bolted on before launch. We write contracts to be audited and build apps to be attacked — secure from the first commit.
Do you work remotely?
Yes. We're remote-first and work with founders and teams across crypto, DeFi, and fintech, wherever they are.
